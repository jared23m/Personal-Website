Here is a little more about Jared's various interests:

Web Development

After Jared took various low-level programming courses at his
university, he began to take an interest in coding, and more
specifically, web design. He is currently working to get his
full stack web development certification through FullStack
Academy, partnering with the University of Illinois at
Chicago.

Music Performance and Production

Jared began playing ukulele at the age of 9, and moved towards
guitar and bass guitar performance once entering high school.
He has performed at a number of venues, including Martyrs' and
the Illinois Insitute of Technology. After college, Jared
began to teach himself how to record, edit, mix, and master
his own music which he releases under the alias Silver Man
Walking.

Mentalism

Jared has had an interest in card magic ever since he was in
kindergarten, performing at birthday parties from as early as
fourth grade. In his adult life, he has transitioned into
mentalism, which uses a combination of psychology, suggestion,
hypnosis, body language and classic mental magic techniques
in order to make it seem like he can read and predict thoughts.
